About Laura

Laura is a current undergraduate student at the University of Miami, studying political science and history, with minors in English and journalism. In her free time, she loves to travel with her friends and family, whether it be short trips to staple locations in Florida, like St.Augustine, or international vacations to countries and islands including:

  • Sweden
  • Switzerland
  • England
  • Spain
  • Russia
  • France
  • Germany
  • Italy
  • and more!

Laura has been traveling since 2002 when she first moved to Florida, where she resides now, from Buenos Aires, Argentina at the mere age of two. Since then, Laura and her family have visited approximately 20 countries, most of which are loctated in the Carribean and the European continent.

Despite all the places she has visited, she particularly enjoys traveling to places with profound cultural history. As a result, her favorite places she has visited has been:

  • Greece
  • Estonia
  • Cayman Islands
