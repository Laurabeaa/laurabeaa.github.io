Breakdown of Areas of Law

Technology Law

Technology law focuses on the intersection between the application of technologies and its implications on various industries around the world. This area of law is primarily interested in understanding the coplexities, challenges, and conflicts that arise from regulating information, technolofical software, and more. One of the most important topics that lawyers in this field are currently analyzing is the advent of artifical intelligence and its impact on cybersecurity and national security operations both domestically and abroad.

Public Interest Law

Public interest law is an area of law that focuses on addressing community needs, particularly those among disadvantaged and marginalized groups of people. Types of cases that public interest law intersects with includes hostile work environments, voting disenfranchisement, prisoners' issues, homelessness and housing, reproductive rights issuesm and more.

Immigration Law

Immigration law focuses on the regulation of individuals' citizenship, residency, asylum status, and more. This area of law specifically manages how individuals in a given country can reside so as to be binded to the rights and obligations of the respective country. Immigration law helps to establish a legal pathway for foreign nationals whom are seeking job opportunities, family whom is seeking visitation rights, or individuals seeking refuge from violence, turmoil, or challenges abroad.
